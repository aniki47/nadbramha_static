Clean City Initiative

Event Images

"Because we want to ensure we welcome our beloved Ganapati Bappa to a clean city every year!"

With awareness of social responsibility, Nadbrahma Trust in association with Janamitra Foundation headed by Raghavendra (Bapu) Mankar along with the cleaning workers of PMC has been actively participating in the “Swach Bharat Abhiyan” initiative by our Hon’ble Prime Minister Narendra Modi. Nadbrahma trust has religiously accomplished this undertaking for the past 2 consecutive years. Every year, after the conclusion of idol immersion of the last Ganapati Mandal on Laxmi road, Nadbrahma trust along with its associates has been prompt in sweeping, collecting and picking up rags and litter from the entire stretch of Laxmi road, starting from Belbaug chowk to Alka Talkies chowk. The participation of our Mayor Prashant Jagtap in this activity was noteworthy and encouraging for all of us. We thank the participating citizens and hope to attract more volunteers each year.
