About Us

"NADBRAHMA DHOL TASHA DHWAJ PATHAK TRUST, PUNE" was established in the year 2011 and is registered under the Charitable Trust Act(1860) in 2014. We are also the first group to officially register our unique logo under the Intellectual Property Act of not only India but also that of the UK. Nadbrahma was started with a modest 60 “Dhol” (Maharashtrian double-headed drums), 15 “Tashe” (Maharashtrian kettledrums) and a team of 50 experienced shoulders. Since its inception, Nadbrahma has focused on establishing a milestone in this traditional folk art and related social activities. With relentless efforts, the organization has grown by multiples. We are now equipped with 160 Dhol, 40 Tashe and other related instruments. We are also supported by a Dhwaj (Maratha victory flag) Pathak with a team of 80 Members. Our total strength has now crossed the count of 500 members. Our members are aged between 6 to 70 years. Today, we all work as one family of 500 people tied together by a common cause.

Since its inception, Nadbrahma has performed at more than 100 Ganeshotsav festivals, Navaratri festivals and various commercial events. The group has been long associated with renowned Ganpati Mandals like “Akhil Mandai Mandal”, “Tambdi Jogeshwari Mandal” and “Twashta Kasar Mandal” to name a few. We are rich in terms of committed manpower of hundreds of devoted youth.

NADBRAHMA has a disciplined three tier management system.

  1.  Board of trustees
  2.  Management Committee
  3.  Activity circles

All the activities go through this stream-lined system of decentralization of power. Projects are allotted to individual activity circles so as to optimize work quality and also enhance their leadership and management skills with backing from management committee and trustees.
