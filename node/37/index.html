Traffic Rules Awareness

Event Images

"Our belief that law-abiding citizens are a true mark of a great city"

Pune is a historically, culturally and educationally enriched city housing one of the biggest IT hubs in the country. And yet it is marred by an undisciplined traffic situation which questions the responsibility, civic sense and lawfulness of its citizens. Each one of us certainly agrees that this image must change. To bring about a revolutionary change in our mindset, we urge all of you to join us in forming the longest possible human chain and taking a united oath to abide by traffic rules and regulations. Nadbrahma Trust in association with Raghavendra (Bapu) Mankar & Friends will extend their support to Pune City Traffic Police in this mission. Let us vow to make Pune a city of rule-abiding citizens!

Date: 6th Oct 2016

Time: 10:00 am

Venue: SamadhanChowk
